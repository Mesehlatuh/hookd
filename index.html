Problem Statement

Topic Points

Recreational (main point)
  • Lack of knowledge of fishing spots and fish species
  • Lack of experienced based fishing knowledge
  • Lack of location based fishing knowledge
  • Uncertainty of catching*
  • Lack of public influence

Environmental (Industrial Fishing)
  • Catching 2.7 TRILLIONS of fish every year
  • Produces 46% of the pacific garbage patch
  • Diminishing many species due to bycatch (whales, dolphins, sea turtles, etc)
  • Wiping out 3.9 billion acres of sea floor per year
  • Only 5% of the ocean is protected and 90% of it stills allows fishing

For more information refer to SEASPIRACY

Solution

Website & App

  1. Location or fish species based fishing day planning system
  2. Provides guidance to reach location and location specific information (weather, temperature, tide level, accessibility, infrastructure, videos and photos) in real time
  3. Provides bait and fishing technique specific for the fish species or the location (video tutorials and photos)
  4. Provides fishing equipment recommendations for purchase and delivery prior to planned fishing date
  5. Premium membership includes quarterly equipment package and unlimited access to all location and fish species information*
  6. Invite general public to practice sustainable and recreational fishing and consequently reduce the impact of industrial fishing

Minimum Value Product MVP

Market

  • Recreational anglers of all levels - 3.4 million (2003, 2005 survey)
    • “Recreational fishing is anticipated to grow in a significant way attributing to the rising spending on angling vacations, wherein people prefer spending their vacation angling at some of the most popular fishing destinations.”
  • Fishing equipment market
    • Approx. 14,000 million in 2019 expected to grow 17,000 million by 2027
